Experience Luxurious Top Floor, Oceanfront, Corner Condo @Horizons

Daytona Beach Oceanfront Condo Florida

Condo, 3 Bedrooms, 2 Baths

18th floor - Upscale Oceanfront vacation paradise condominium sleeps 6, (up-charge for additional guests, children welcome)- located one mile north of Daytona Beach pier and about ten to fifteen minutes from Daytona Speedway.

SEND THE KIDS BACK TO SCHOOL SPECIAL 8/15-9/15, AND GET GRANDMA TO BABYSIT, End unit facing east (the ocean), south, and west. The 400 square ft,wrap around balcony gives you a perfect 360 degree unobstructed views of the ocean, the river and the entire Daytona Beach. The balcony from the living/ dining area wraps around the east and south side of the unit, with access from the master suite, and a private balcony to the west.

Absolute privacy, fitness room, sauna, and oceanfront pool, cable TV in every room. Computer, printer and wifi.
Living room/ Dining room has 3D TV, wifi, surround sound, cd, dvd, vhs, Video library. Pub table overlooking the beach. Dining table seats 8. Master suite with a king bed, easy chair, separate spacious dressing area, bath and large closet, CD library, TV, and surround sound.

For maximum privacy, the guest bedrooms and full bath with large shower are down a separated hallway.
Large guest bedroom with a queen bed, easy chair, TV DVD VHS, with river and beach view. Beach towels, umbrella, chairs, cooler bags, beach bags, blanket, and boogie boards. Even rain ponchos and jackets for a rainy day excursion.
Third bedroom with two bunk-beds, full size on the bottom and twin size on the top, and PlayStation, childrens video library, games and jig saw puzzles.
Fully equipped kitchen with all needed cookware, ice maker, spices, coffee, flour, sugar, serving ware and utensils, even cookbooks. All you need is food and your bathing suit. Publix the local supermarket delivers your order.

This condo is in the award winning Horizons Condominium on the 18th floor end unit with 1750 interior square feet . The view to the south is toward the Boardwalk and Kennedy Space Center. The guest bedroom views the ocean looking north as well as the river to the west. This view is not available in the 2 bedroom condos.

The property is very secure. 24/7 camera surveillance, and all entries to the building and the property are locked. Keywords: spacious, residential building.
It was built as a home, not a timeshare or hotel. This is our home with all of the amenities. I rent it when I am not here. Ultra clean. luxuriously furnished.

There are few condos in this area of the beach, there is not much beach traffic this far north, and the beach is cleaner and quieter . An an easy walk along the Atlantic Ocean to Daytona Beach Pier, entertainment and shopping. Off street parking on the premises by permit.


PLEASE NOTE: trailers cannot be parked here.


DAYTONA VIEWS: enjoy the beach views and sunrise from the balcony, and the sunset with a glass of wine from the private balcony off the master bedroom.
